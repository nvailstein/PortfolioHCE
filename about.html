About me

Software Engineer graduating in May 2020 from Monmouth University.

Knowing how systems work and developing ways to build and modify them has been my passion since my first Lego set. I have spent countless hours increasing my understanding on how systems work and what I can do to change them. I believe that knowledge is the driving factor to success and I am looking for an opportunity to both expand and use mine.

Working with computers is both a passion and hobby for me. I like to see through the whole development cycle of a project, from gathering requirements to deployment. I am very strong in a small team environment and am open to new work environments. I have developed numerous projects both small and large at my time at university.

Outside of software development I have many hobbies which have taught me a lot about myself and the world around me. I have been a pole vaulter for eight years and am currently on the track and field team at Monmouth University. I have been a musician for around twelve years playing both guitar and bass. I also have a passion for cars, machine operating, magic the gathering, and video games.

I have work experience outside of my field which consisted of a summer job with Sylvan Motels Inc. under the leadership of John Hurler Sr and Andrew Hurler. At this job I worked as both a receptionist for two years and as the night auditor concurrently during the second year. I am no stranger to hard work and during my time there I averaged over fifty hours per week during most months working there.

Skills/Interests: Java, C/C++, Python, C#, HTML, CSS. Swift, SQL, Ruby on Rails, and Git
